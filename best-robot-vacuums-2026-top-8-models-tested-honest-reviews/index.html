Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are robot vacuums worth it in 2026?

Yes. Modern robot vacuums handle daily cleaning very effectively and significantly reduce the time spent on floor maintenance.

2. What is the best robot vacuum overall?

The Roborock S9 / S8 Pro Ultra remains the most balanced option in terms of cleaning power, navigation, and automation.

3. Which robot vacuum is best for pet hair?

Shark Matrix AI Ultra and Roomba Combo j9+ are among the strongest performers for pet hair removal.

4. Is a self-emptying dock worth it?

Yes. It reduces maintenance significantly, often requiring attention only every few weeks instead of daily.

5. Do robot vacuums actually mop well?

Premium models with rotating or vibrating mop systems (like Dreame, Roborock, Narwal, Ecovacs) can clean floors very effectively. Basic models only provide light surface wiping.

6. LiDAR or camera navigation?

LiDAR is generally better for mapping and efficiency. Camera systems are better at recognizing obstacles. Some premium models combine both.

7. Can a robot vacuum replace a normal vacuum?

Not completely. It handles daily maintenance, but a traditional vacuum is still useful for deep cleaning and hard-to-reach areas.

8. How often should I run it?

Most homes benefit from 3–7 runs per week depending on pets and foot traffic.

9. How long do they last?

Typically 4–7 years with proper maintenance and part replacement.

10. Which brand has the best app?
