Best Pet Cameras 2026: Top 7 Smart Cameras for Dogs and Cats Tested & Honest Reviews

Leaving your pet home alone has never been easy. Whether you’re at work, traveling, or simply running errands, it’s natural to wonder what your dog or cat is doing while you’re away. Are they sleeping peacefully, getting into trouble, or waiting by the door for you to come home?

That’s exactly why pet cameras have become one of the most popular smart home devices in 2026. Today’s models offer much more than a simple live video feed. The best pet cameras 2026 can notify you when your pet is active, let you talk to them through two-way audio, dispense treats remotely, and even use AI-powered detection to recognize barking, meowing, or unusual behavior. For many pet owners, these features provide peace of mind and help reduce separation anxiety—for both pets and humans.

A quality best dog camera can also help identify destructive behavior, monitor pets recovering from illness, check in on anxious animals, or simply capture those funny everyday moments you would otherwise miss. If you’re looking for the best pet camera with treat dispenser, advanced AI tracking, or an affordable indoor camera for everyday monitoring, choosing the right model can make a significant difference.

To create this guide, we evaluated every camera using the factors that matter most in daily use, including:

  • Video quality and field of view
  • Night vision performance
  • Two-way audio clarity
  • Treat dispensing reliability
  • AI-powered pet detection and smart alerts
  • Mobile app usability
  • Cloud subscription value
  • Smart home compatibility
  • Overall price-to-performance ratio
